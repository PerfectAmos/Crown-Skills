The dynamics of employment, job opportunities, and work culture are constantly evolving. Majority of the working professionals need to be skilled, reskilled, and upskilled in the next years to meet the demands of the dynamic global labor market. Considering the urgency for skilled human resources in both national and international job markets, Crown Institute aims to design and provide market-oriented skills development training for employment promotion. Our goal is to connect job seekers with job providers and equip them with the most up-to-date skills in various sectors to enhance employability.
